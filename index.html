The Discovery

it was real either

I first discovered Akiya — Japan's abandoned or vacant houses — through social media. At first, I dismissed it as too good to be true. A house in Japan for the price of a used car?

Over two years, I researched obsessively. I learned the process, the pitfalls, the language barriers, the overpriced middlemen specifically targeting foreigners. Then I bought my house.
